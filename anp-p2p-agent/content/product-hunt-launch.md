## Tagline (60 chars max)

## Description
MapleBridge.io connects North American buyers with verified Chinese manufacturers using semantic AI matching — not keyword search.

Instead of browsing endless supplier directories, post your sourcing request and let AI match you with the right factory. Built specifically for Amazon FBA sellers, Shopify brands, and Canadian/US importers.

**Key Features:**
- Semantic AI matching (intent-based, not keyword-based)
- Suppliers cross-verified from Alibaba, Global Sources, Made-in-China, DHgate
- North America compliance matching (FDA, Health Canada, CPSC, FCC, IC)
- Small batch friendly — MOQs as low as 100 units
- Free for buyers — no platform fees, no commissions

**Who it's for:**
- Amazon FBA sellers looking for factories that handle FNSKU labeling and direct-to-warehouse shipping
- Shopify/D2C brands sourcing private-label products at low MOQs
- Canadian importers leveraging the tariff advantage (no Section 301 tariffs)
- Any North American business sourcing products from China

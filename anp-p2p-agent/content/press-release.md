# PRESS RELEASE

## MapleBridge.io Launches AI-Powered Supplier Matching Platform for North American Buyers Sourcing from China

**Toronto, Canada — March 25, 2026** — MapleBridge.io, a new AI-powered B2B platform, today announced the launch of its supplier matching service designed specifically for North American businesses sourcing products from Chinese manufacturers.

Unlike traditional B2B marketplaces such as Alibaba and Global Sources that rely on keyword-based directory search, MapleBridge.io uses semantic AI to match buyer sourcing requests with verified Chinese manufacturers based on product requirements, compliance needs, and order specifications.

"The traditional model of browsing supplier directories is broken for North American importers," said Jacob Huang, founder of MapleBridge.io. "A Canadian buyer needs suppliers who understand Health Canada registration and bilingual labeling requirements. An Amazon FBA seller needs factories that handle FNSKU labeling and ship directly to fulfillment centers. Generic directories don't filter for any of this — our AI does."

### Key Platform Features

- **Semantic AI Matching**: Buyers post sourcing requests in natural language; the AI matches them with relevant manufacturers based on intent, not keywords
- **Cross-Platform Supplier Verification**: Manufacturer data is aggregated and verified across Alibaba, Global Sources, Made-in-China.com, and DHgate
- **North America Compliance Matching**: Built-in filtering for FDA, Health Canada, CPSC, FCC, IC, and other regulatory standards
- **Small Batch Support**: Minimum order quantities as low as 100-500 units, serving the growing Amazon FBA and Shopify seller market
- **Free for Buyers**: No platform fees, no commissions, no subscription required

### Market Opportunity

The China-to-North America B2B trade market represents over $500 billion in annual import volume. The rise of Amazon FBA (with over 2 million active sellers) and Shopify (powering over 4 million stores globally) has created a new class of small-to-medium importers who need efficient supplier discovery but lack the resources for traditional sourcing agents.

MapleBridge.io addresses this gap by combining AI-powered matching with compliance-aware filtering — a combination not offered by existing B2B marketplaces.

### Technical Innovation

The platform features a dual-language AI engine: Chinese-context queries are processed by QWEN for accurate understanding of manufacturing terminology, while English-context requests use OpenAI models for optimal North American buyer intent parsing. This bilingual approach significantly improves matching accuracy compared to single-language systems.
